Education

2008-2012
bachelor of arts

"Think one person can change the world? So do we."

Entering Oberlin College I knew that I wanted to change the world; the only uncertainty was how. Then I took a course in art history and immediately fell in love. Medieval art and illuminated manuscripts were of particular interest but I also studied East Asian art, Islamic art, and arts of the Italian Renaissance.

After some internship experiences, I decided upon a career in the museum field and sought other classes to help bolster my knowledge. I took a practicum in museum education, several courses in grant writing and another practicum in exhibitions. These courses transformed my understanding of art history and helped me to apply it to the museum world.
